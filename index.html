Starting Over

Hey how you doing? Welcome to my own personal dev blog. This was long overdue as I started trying to learn how to design websites a couple years ago but I eventually put it down to pursue other things. Life kinda throws these different pathways and skills for you to learn and walk on but unfortunately I did not choose web design as one of those. Who knows where I could have been now if I chose to work on it then.

Anyways, I decided to dust off the old C drive on my laptop and saw some unfinished websites and remembered that I took a course on webdesign that I have not finished. I thought about it and figured what if I take the course all the way to the end? So now I'm starting my web development journey from scratch just to see how much I remember. It's gonna be long and messy but hopefully I can make something out of this by the end of this year.

Keep track of this Date: May 16, 2024. This is where it all began (again)
